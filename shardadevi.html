About SDDC

SDDC was established in 2010, under the aegis of the Smt. Sharda Devi Educational Charitable Trust. The aim was to establish the
institution that provide access to higher education of quality and to show a never ending commitment to nurturing and creating
students who are equipped to be world citizens. With more than 13+ years of experience in training future Leaders, entrepreneurs and
managers, it has emerged as one of the distinguished institutions in the Bundelkhand Region. The college is housed in a sprawling
campus with a lush green, pollution free environment, by far one of the best and greenest campuses in the region.
